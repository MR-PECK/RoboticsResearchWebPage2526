Project Overview

The Mission: Work in collaborative research teams to investigate a specific sector of the robotics industry (e.g., Medical, Agricultural, Manufacturing, Space Exploration). You will bridge the gap between technical innovation and career reality.

Core Topics

  • Current Applications: How robots are used today.
  • Cutting-Edge Tech: AI, Swarm, Soft Robotics.
  • Career Pathways: Salaries & Job Growth.
  • Skill Acquisition: What to learn in High School.

Deliverables

  • Technical Paper: Formal IEEE format (Word Doc).
  • Academic Poster: Visual gallery walk (24x36").
  • Oral Pitch: 2-minute "Shark Tank" style pitch.

üìù Daily Workspace

Day 1: Kickoff & Selection

Learning Target: Define roles & select industry sector.

Activity: Sign team contract. Review IEEE examples. Choose sector (Medical, Ag, etc.).

Day 2: "Current State" Research

Learning Target: Identify current robotic applications.

Activity: Find the standard robot used in your field today.

Day 3: Data Analysis (Math)

Learning Target: Interpret labor statistics & salary data.

Activity: Find quantitative data (BLS.gov) on job growth.

Day 4: Drafting (Intro & Tech)

Learning Target: Synthesize technical research into writing.

Day 5: Drafting (Careers)

Learning Target: Map specific career pathways.

Day 6: Poster Visualization

Learning Target: Apply visual hierarchy to data.

Day 7: Peer Review

Learning Target: Evaluate peer work for accuracy.

Day 8: Final Polish

Learning Target: Finalize formatting and citations.

Day 9: Pitch Rehearsal

Learning Target: Practice oral communication.

Day 10: Gallery Walk

Learning Target: Professional presentation.
